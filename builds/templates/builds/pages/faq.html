FAQ

Frequently asked questions

A short launch FAQ for the build-order archive and related Prostagma? tools.

What is a build order?

A build order is an opening plan for the early game. It usually tells you what to gather, when to build key structures, and what timing or strategic transition the opening is aiming for.

Are these builds beginner friendly?

Some are beginner friendly, while others assume you already understand basic AoM mechanics. The site focuses on making each build easier to read, even when the strategy itself is advanced.

Where do the builds come from?

The current library is based on public community resources, including the DoD build-order collection. The pages have been imported and reformatted so they are easier to browse by pantheon, major god, and opening type.

Why do some gods have more builds than others?

The available public build-order library is uneven. Some gods have many documented openings, while others have fewer public examples. The site reflects what is available and will improve as more good builds are added.

Can I submit a build order?

Public submissions are planned, but the launch version is curated first so the build library starts from a stable foundation.

Is Prostagma? official?

No. Prostagma? is an independent fan project and is not affiliated with Microsoft, World’s Edge, Forgotten Empires, Xbox Game Studios, or the official Age of Empires / Age of Mythology teams.
