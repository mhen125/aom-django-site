About Prostagma?

Age of Mythology build orders, preserved and made readable.

Prostagma? is an unofficial Age of Mythology: Retold resource for build orders, live activity tools, lobby browsing, and community-focused game information.

Why this exists

AoM has a lot of useful community knowledge, but much of it is scattered across fragile pages, Discord posts, videos, spreadsheets, and older resources that are not always easy to browse or preserve.

The goal of Prostagma? is simple: make useful openings easier to find, easier to read, and less dependent on links that can disappear without warning.

Build order sources

The launch build library is based on public community build orders, including the DoD build-order collection and other credited community resources. The builds are imported, organized by major god, and reformatted for readability.

Original strategy credit belongs to the players and communities who created those openings. Prostagma? exists to preserve and improve access to that knowledge.

Unofficial project

Prostagma? is an independent fan project. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Microsoft, World’s Edge, Forgotten Empires, Xbox Game Studios, or the official Age of Empires / Age of Mythology teams.
